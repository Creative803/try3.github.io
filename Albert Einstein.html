Albert Einstein

About

Albert Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany. He was a theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity. It is one of the main things of modern physics. He is very famous for his mass-energy equivalence formula E = mc², which is labelled as the world's most famous equation.
